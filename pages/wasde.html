World Agricultural Supply and Demand Estimates (WASDE)

The latest WASDE report was released on February 9th, 2021. Information contained in the report can be found below. Click a crop name below to jump to that crop.

Corn

April projections for U.S. corn call for greater feed and residual use, increased corn used for ethanol production and larger exports. Endings stocks are lowered to 1,352 million bushels. The average farm price remains constant at $4.30.

Soybeans

April projections for soybeans saw lower crush and higher exports. Soybean exports are raised to reflect record exports through the first half of the marketing year. Soybean ending stocks are projected at 120 million bushels. The average farm price remains constant at $11.15.
