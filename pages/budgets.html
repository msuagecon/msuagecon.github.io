2021 Planning Budgets

The Agricultural Economics Department has released 2021 Planning Budgets. These publications provides economic and technical information in the form of planning budgets for major crops produced by Mississippi farmers. A multidisciplinary approach involving researchers and extension personnel was used to determine production practices and input quantities, and to estimate costs and returns for each crop type.
